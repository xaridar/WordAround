Welcome to WordAround!

To play, just type some letters - they'll display in the circle.

Your goal is to guess the hidden word with as little information as possible. The less letters you need to guess a word, the more points you earn.

To start, 4 letters of the hidden word are given. Try to guess the missing letter, then press GUESS (or enter on your keyboard) to make a guess.

If you guessed correctly, you will earn points and a new word will fill in. The only letters you will start with are those shared between the old and new word.

If you guess incorrectly, another random letter will fill in, giving you an opportunity to guess again.

If you still can't guess the word when there's only one letter left, the game is over.
